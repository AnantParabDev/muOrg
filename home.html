Description of Few Genres

1.Pop
-Pop music originated in the late 1950s as a derivation of traditional pop, combining other musical genres fashionable at the time.
-It is a genre of popular music characterized by catchy melodies and hooks, simple harmonies, and a focus on the singer or band.
Pop
2.Rock
-Rock music is a style born in the 1960s as a derivative of rock and roll, characterized by the use of complex melodies and rhythms, with fairly fixed instrumentation (electric guitar, electric bass, keyboard, and drums) and an energetic rhythm underlined by the power of the bass and drums.
Rock
3.Hip-Hop
- Hip-hop music is a dance music style born in the United States in the 1970s as a derivative of funk, characterized by its electronic base and association with alternative manifestations such as break dance or graffiti.
-It uses a technique named "Rapping" which is poetry spoken rhythmically.

Hip-Hop
